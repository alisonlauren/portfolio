React E-Commerce App

Soap & Co

An E-commerce application built in React. This app allows password protected users to browse soaps, add to cart, input their shipping information, and safely pay via PayPal or Credit Card. Additionally, the app alows users to change their password, name or email. They can also see all of their order history, whether their items have been paid or shipped yet. I created an administrator side as well, so that they can keep track of order history. I typically love Front-End Development, so that's I decided to dive deep into putting all of my energy in server side development. I wanted to make sure the "bones" of this project were strong before I dove further in design. The styling came from CSS exclusively.

Ecommerce Site

Client Ecommerce Project

The humble beginnings of a new client project. Will be written in CSS, HTML, and Javascript. The customer is looking for a ecommerce site that will enable them to sell products online and setup payment via PayPal. Additionally, she's looking for a design that showcases the homemade quality of her handsoaps. The challenges I will overcome in this project are creating a database of products, and the functionality to handle inventory. This has been a fun and collaborative project. I always aim to exceed customer expectations and go above and beyond.
